ABOUT OUR SALON FOR DOGS

Caring

We believe your canine companions are an integral part of your family and that is how we treat every pet that comes to our salon. We truly enjoy meeting each new pet and getting to know their individual personalities and grooming needs. We don't believe in getting upset, frustrated or rough with pets, period. We desire that each dog leave our salon having had an excellent experience that leaves them with a sense of belonging and calm on their next visit.

Cleanliness

We pride ourselves on the cleanliness of our salon and equipment and, in fact, it is one of the kind compliments we hear most often from our customers. We take great care to sanitize all salon service equipment between each pet, ensuring that your dog will be groomed with tools that have been thoroughly disinfected before being used on them. We hold that same high standard to all aspects of cleaning every surface of the Beauti-Fur salon, ensuring a santiary condition of the floor, the grooming table, the grooming brushes and blades that used on your dog.

Customer Service

In our opinion, great customer service starts with understanding communication, whereby, we focus on listening to your wants and assessing your dog's needs. We start by asking each new client exactly how they would like their pet to be trimmed and even showing them pictures of similar styles so that you, the pet parent, will have a clear visual idea of what to expect the cut to look like in the finished product. Occasionally, we will have to explain why a certain style may not work for a particular dog. We are frequently surprised at how often customers have told me that no other groomers have taken the time to patiently listen and make educated suggestions before servicing their dog. We truly believe most mistakes can be avoided through clear and simple communication.

Continuing Education

It is unfortunate that the grooming industry is currently unregulated allowing anyone to become a pet groomer or grooming shop owner with little or no training, experience and specific skill sets. This is a very unsettling fact, not knowing with whom you can entrust to provide grooming care and service for your canine companion. Rest assured that we attend a minimum of two continuing education seminars a year to stay current on new grooming proceedures and dog care products, ensuring that our advanced techiques result in a better, faster, safer and more enjoyable experience for your pet.

DOG DE-SHEDDING
MUD BATH

The de-shedding Mud is applied to your dog after a regular bath and has a sit time of 10 minutes. During that time we provide your dog with a relaxing hand massage to work the mud thoroughly through the coat to loosen the dead hair. After which your dog is rinsed, dried and a de-shedding tool is used to finish up. Our De-Shedding Mud is all-natural and detergent-free. It will rejuvenate your dog’s skin and coat and help relieve and prevent excessive shedding while promoting healthy coat and skin.

Special Note: While there is no product or service available on the market that will completely stop shedding 100%, this particualr product and service will greatly reduce the amount of shedding by approximately 90%. This service is most efective when performed on a monthly basis to coincide with the natural cycle of hair growth.
